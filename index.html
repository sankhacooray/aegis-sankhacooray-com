Aegis keeps an emergency medical summary — conditions, medications, allergies, and critical alerts — that you can show or print for emergency personnel in seconds. Your data lives in your own Google account; only you can see it.

  • Aegis only ever touches the one file it creates for you — never the rest of your Drive.
  • Your record stays in your Google Drive. We can't read other people's data, and they can't read yours.
  • Edit it anytime as a normal Google Sheet, or print it as a PDF.
Aegis is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always verify information with the patient or their physician where possible.
